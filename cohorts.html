There comes a point in your coding journey when it just doesn’t cut it to show your partner, sister, or roommate for the I’ve-lost-counth time if your site looks better with a certain div shifted 10 more pixels to the left or not.

But what if you gave yourself scheduled, quality time each week with your creative coding project?

♥ Time that was carved out uniquely for you and your keyboard clicks.

♥ Time that allowed you to focus wholeheartedly on your project instead of feeling that slight twinge of guilt for not paying attention to your dog who keeps dropping his favorite toy at your feet.

Are you ready to finally give yourself the time to create the coding projects that are constantly taking root and then promptly being moved to the recycle bin of your brain when life just gets in the way?
